What We Talked About: Topic Summary üéØ

Today, the lesson primarily focused on the Present Perfect Continuous (PPC). You learned that PPC is used for actions that started in the past and are still continuing now (e.g., "I've been studying all morning") or for actions that recently stopped but have a clear result now (e.g., "She's been cleaning, that's why the room is clean").
